Projects

Waiting For Words

Waiting for Words is a new song cycle with music and orchestrations by Josua Lutian and lyrics and direction by Katie Monteleone. The song cycle features eleven original songs that explore the ins and outs of college experiences; the characters go through the process of trying to figure out who they are, how they fit into the world, and how they might find the words to express themselves. The song cycle will feature twenty-three talented Colby performers and musicians and will premiere in Colby’s Lorimer Chapel on April 13, 2018.

Lost With You

Lost With You is a new musical that tells the story of a family of four who never seem to be in sync with one another. Emulating her mother as a successful owner of a local iced tea company, Juliette sells lemonade in the hopes of one day getting out of the small town and becoming somebody. Meanwhile, Juliette’s older brother Jerome spends his days playing chess alone, never having felt like he belonged anywhere. When a new boy named Nick moves into town, everything changes. Jerome suddenly finds himself lost in the possibility of having met someone who makes him feel something real for the first time. A heartwarming story about family and acceptance, Lost With You explores what it means to find happiness in a place you never thought you would.

Lemonade Stand

Lemonade Stand premiered on April 15th, 2016, at Colby College as a workshop performance. The song cycle tells the story of a dysfunctional family of goofy characters who own a lemonade stand business. When a new family moves in down the street and opens a competing iced tea business, chaos breaks out. Filled with fun music and an eclectic cast of characters, Lemonade Stand explores the ideas of acceptance, family, and belonging. The show was later reimagined and revised into the musical Lost With You, which premiered at Colby in February, 2017.
